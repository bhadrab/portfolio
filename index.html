Electrical & Computer Engineer

Hi! I'm Bhadra Bejoy,

I'm a senior at Cornell University, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a passion for engineering to create a better world.

I enjoy the world of robotics, through my research experience and course work, I've been able to combine creativity and engineering to bring machines to life. Whether it's designing PCBs, simulating, writing embedded code, or fine-tuning the mechanics, I thrive on the challenges that robotics presents and am excited for its future.

I'm equally interested in the electric vehicle industry - a field that holds the promise of transforming transportation while reducing our environmental footprint. My internship with Rivian and involvement with Cornell Electric Vehicles has allowed me to explore interests in PCB design, power electronics, testing and validation, and analog circuit design.

When I'm not in the EE lab, you can find me drawing on my iPad, cooking, designing and sewing clothes, or dancing:)
