Hi! I'm Bhadra Bejoy,

I'm a senior at Cornell University, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a passion for creating technology solutions that contribute to a better world.

I enjoy the world of robotics, through my research experience and course work, I've been able to combine creativity and engineering prowess to bring machines to life. Whether it's designing PCBs, writing embedded code, or fine-tuning the mechanics, I thrive on the challenges that robotics presents and am excited for its future.

My internship with Rivian and involvement with Cornell Electric Vehicles has allowed me to explore interests in PCB design, power electronics, testing and validation, and analog circuit design.
